WDD131 Final Project Site Plan

Site Name and Logo

I chose the name "Bioinformatics Protein: Shaped Functionality", because bioinformatics helps us know what a protein does based on it's shape.
Logo: laptop

Site Purpose

Introduction to (what Bioinformatics is and) how Bioinformatics helps us visualize protein 3D structures/shapes and predict how structures function and how can help with drug discovery and development.

Scenarios

“Scenarios are questions asked by a site visitor who represents the organization's target audience. Who is target audience?”
Target audience is college undergraduate students (and maybe high school students) and curious biochemistry or molecular biology liking adults.
At least 2 scenarios :
What is Bioinformatics?
Why is visualizing proteins important? (Structure predicts what protein will do)
What does a protein structure look like in 3D?
How can I get or find Bioinformatics Software/programs?

Color Schema

  • Green lighter - Primary
  • Blue - Secondary. Back up is a light brown.
  • Text color(s): white, cream white, or (light) gray
